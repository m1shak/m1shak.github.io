About me

My name is Mykhailo, and I am 19 years old. For the past three years, I have been living in Switzerland. I currently reside in Ostermundigen and attend the BFF Berufsvorbereitung program.

In 2022, due to the war in Ukraine, my family and I were forced to leave our home. We initially moved to Wichtrach, where we received immense support from a kind Swiss family. Their help during such a difficult time meant a lot to us, and we are deeply grateful for their generosity and care. Later, we relocated to Ostermundigen, where we are now building a new chapter of our lives.

In my free time, I enjoy meeting with friends and improving my German and English language skills. My hobbies include playing football and table tennis, which I am truly passionate about.

Living in Switzerland has been an enriching experience, and I am always eager to explore new opportunities for personal growth and learning.
